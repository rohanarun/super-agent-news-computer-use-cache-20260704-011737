How Super’s computer-use cache works

1. Observe

Super performs the workflow on a real computer: logins, navigation, forms, and confirmations.

2. Verify

Successful steps are validated and scoped to the task and environment.

3. Reuse

Future runs replay safe actions from the computer-use cache instead of rediscovering them.

4. Improve

When apps change, Super adapts and refreshes cached steps.
